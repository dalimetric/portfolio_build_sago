About Me

I’ve always been fueled by imagination. I would often draw and create my own worlds as a kid, and I decided to follow that path by enrolling in Animation at Sheridan College.

That turned that into a fantastic freelance career in Illustration, working with clients such as Air Canada, Ritzenhoff, and The Globe & Mail

As tech expanded into all media, I took advantage by infusing these visual skills with usability and design. I shifted to a UI Artist role with Gameloft, and ultimately moved into UX and product-thinking. I’ve been bringing value as a Product Designer for 10+ years now, with a focus on creativity and learning.

Some of my biggest inspirations on that front: Lego for it’s inclusion and emotion, Minecraft for the sense of free form world-building, and Nintendo LABO for using boxes ‘outside of the box’. :)
